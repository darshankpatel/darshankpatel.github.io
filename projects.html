GSM Based Smart UPS Battery Charging System With Precise Battery Backup Estimation

Hitachi Hi-Rel Power Electronics Pvt. Ltd.

February 2018 - April 2018

UPS stand for Uninterruptable Power Supply. It is one kind of supply system which is connected between electric grid and consumer. In UPS it needs electric hardware components and batteries. Mostly Uninterruptable Power Supply is used to provide emergency power supply such places like hospitals, computer network, communication system, industrial processes etc.
Now days in market various types of UPS available but still there are some features are missing. So, we work on it and we want to make a smart UPS system with battery backup estimation as loads are changes. It means we use microcontroller and LCD display to show the life of batteries or backup batteries on LCD display. So, easily any workers can understand and take action with in time duration.
Secondary we connect this UPS system with GSM module. So, at every fixed time the text message is sent to connect mobile who works on particular UPS. At the competition of our project we will hopefully to make it reliable and user friendly in functions add compare to other UPS available in market.
